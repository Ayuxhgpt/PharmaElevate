Affiliation, Recognition & Approval

  • D.Pharm: Approved by PCI & AICTE (New Delhi), recognized by State Govt. of UP & affiliated to BTE Lucknow.
  • B.Pharm: Approved by PCI & AICTE (New Delhi), recognized by State Govt. of UP & affiliated to AKTU Lucknow.

About Satyadev College of Pharmacy

Satyadev College is a renowned institution dedicated to academic excellence and holistic development. With a rich legacy of imparting quality education, our college stands as a beacon of knowledge, innovation, and values. Our mission is to empower students with the skills, knowledge, and integrity necessary to excel in their professional careers and contribute meaningfully to society.

Our Vision

To emerge as a leading institution of higher learning, recognized for academic excellence, innovative research, and commitment to societal development. We aim to produce graduates who are not only knowledgeable but also socially responsible and globally competitive.

Our Values

  • Excellence: We strive for academic excellence and continuous improvement.
  • Innovation: We encourage innovation and creativity in our students and faculty.
  • Integrity: We uphold the highest standards of integrity and ethics.
  • Inclusivity: We promote a culture of inclusivity and diversity.

Facilities & Support

The college supports faculty development by providing research facilities. The library holds over 3500 books and subscribes to more than 12 international & national journals. Faculty members are qualified per PCI/AICTE norms. Our future endeavour is to start M.Pharm and Pharm.D courses.
