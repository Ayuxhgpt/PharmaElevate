What is Pharmacy?

Pharmacy is the science and practice concerned with the discovery, preparation, dispensing and proper utilization of medicines. It bridges health sciences and chemical sciences to ensure safe and effective use of pharmaceutical drugs.

  • Medicine formulation & quality control
  • Patient counseling & clinical pharmacy
  • Pharmacovigilance (drug safety)
  • Research & development

Scope of Pharmacy

Career Areas

Hospital Pharmacy, Clinical Research, Pharmaceutical Industry, Regulatory Affairs, Academics, Community Pharmacy, Quality Control and more. Pharmacists can work in research labs, biotech firms, drug regulatory agencies, hospitals and community pharmacies.

Future of Pharmacy (Opportunities)

Pharmacovigilance

Monitoring and analyzing drug safety — a growing field with global demand.

Biotech & Biosimilars

Development of advanced biologics and biosimilar therapies.

AI in Drug Discovery

Using machine learning to speed up drug screening and design.

Telepharmacy & Digital Health

Remote patient counselling, e-prescriptions and digital pharmacy services.

What You Can Do as a Student

  • Pursue internships in industry or hospitals
  • Learn clinical research & pharmacovigilance tools
  • Gain lab skills: formulation, analysis, QC methods
  • Explore data science & bioinformatics for pharma
  • Participate in workshops, conferences and publish small articles
