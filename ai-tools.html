AI Tools for Students — Practical Guide

Overview

Students can use AI tools for literature search, summarisation, flashcard creation, and draft generation. Examples include Semantic Scholar, Jenni, and other literature-review assistants.

Responsible use

  • Always verify AI-generated summaries against primary sources
  • Use AI as an assistant, not a substitute for learning
