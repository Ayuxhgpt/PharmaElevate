AI & Digital Pharma — Applications

Overview

AI and digital technologies accelerate drug discovery, support literature review, enable predictive models, and empower telepharmacy and digital therapeutics. Students can use AI tools to summarise papers, create flashcards, and explore molecule property prediction tools.

Student tools

  • Semantic Scholar / literature AI tools for rapid review
  • AI assistants for summarisation and flashcard generation
  • Cloud notebooks and basic cheminformatics tools (QSAR, similarity search)
