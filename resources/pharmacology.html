Pharmacology Basics — Mechanisms & Receptors

Overview

Pharmacology studies drug actions and mechanisms at molecular targets such as receptors, ion channels, and enzymes. Core concepts include pharmacokinetics (what the body does to the drug) and pharmacodynamics (what the drug does to the body).

Key topics

  • Drug-receptor interactions and affinity/efficacy
  • Pharmacokinetics: absorption, distribution, metabolism, elimination
  • Major drug classes and therapeutic uses
