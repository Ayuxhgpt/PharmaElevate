Clinical Pharmacy — Role & Practice

Overview

Clinical pharmacy focuses on optimizing medication therapy and promoting health outcomes in direct patient care settings. Clinical pharmacists collaborate with physicians and healthcare teams to provide therapeutic recommendations, manage chronic diseases, monitor drug therapy effectiveness, and counsel patients on medication use.

Key responsibilities

  • Medication therapy management and review
  • Adverse drug reaction monitoring and prevention
  • Participating in ward rounds and interdisciplinary teams
  • Patient counselling and medication reconciliation
