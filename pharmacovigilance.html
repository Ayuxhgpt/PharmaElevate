Pharmacovigilance — Drug Safety & Reporting

Overview

Pharmacovigilance is the science and activities related to the detection, assessment, understanding, and prevention of adverse effects or any other drug-related problems. It supports safer use of medicines through reporting systems, signal detection, and regulatory action.

In India: The Pharmacovigilance Programme of India (PvPI) collects and analyzes adverse event reports and helps inform regulatory decisions and safe-use guidance.
