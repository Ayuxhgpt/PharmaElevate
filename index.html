Research & Trends shaping Pharmacy

Pharmacovigilance — Drug Safety

Pharmacovigilance is the science of detecting, assessing and preventing adverse effects and other drug-related problems. Robust PV systems are essential for public safety and post-marketing surveillance. 5

AI in Drug Discovery & Safety

Artificial intelligence accelerates target identification, lead optimization and safety prediction — enabling faster and cheaper drug discovery pipelines. AI is also being applied to pharmacovigilance to detect signals from big data sources. 6

Telepharmacy — Remote Care

Telepharmacy delivers pharmaceutical care remotely (counseling, monitoring, and sometimes dispensing) and helps expand access in underserved areas. Telepharmacy programs are increasingly adopted worldwide. 7

Pharma Careers & India’s Role

India continues to scale its pharma R&D and manufacturing capabilities — fueling job creation in clinical research, manufacturing, regulatory affairs and pharmacovigilance. Recent industry growth and investment are creating more opportunities for pharmacy graduates. 8
