Research & Trends (2025)

Personalized Medicine

Genomic profiling + AI models allow tailored therapies — reduces adverse reactions and improves efficacy for chronic/oncology treatments.

Nanotech Drug Delivery

Nano-carriers (liposomes, polymeric nanoparticles) enhance targeted delivery, cross biological barriers, and lower systemic toxicity.

Biologics & Biosimilars

Monoclonal antibodies, peptide therapeutics, and biosimilars shape modern therapeutics — regulatory and cold-chain challenges remain key.

Green Pharmacy

Sustainable synthesis, solvent reduction, and waste management — pharma industry shifts towards environmentally-friendly processes.

Telepharmacy Expansion

Remote consultations, automated dispensing, and telemonitoring of chronic patients — bridging rural and urban healthcare gaps.

AI-assisted Drug Discovery

Machine learning predicts molecular interactions, optimizes lead compounds, and accelerates early-stage drug development.
