Research & Trends Shaping Pharmacy in 2025

Pharmacovigilance & AI Integration

Transitioning from traditional retrospective analysis to proactive real-time monitoring, AI and Real-World Data enable rapid detection of adverse drug reactions (ADRs), enhancing patient safety and intervention efficiency.

AI in Drug Discovery

AI predicts disease susceptibility, identifies potential drug targets, and accelerates molecular design and clinical property inference, reducing R&D time and costs.

Telepharmacy & Remote Care

Telepharmacy improves access to pharmaceutical care, particularly in rural and underserved regions. The market is projected to grow to $13.57B by 2032.

Pharmacy Education in India

Integration of AI tools in pharmacy curricula enables students to leverage technology for research and learning. Top institutions like Jamia Hamdard have maintained leading positions in NIRF rankings.
