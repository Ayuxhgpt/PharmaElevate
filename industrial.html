Industrial Pharmacy — Formulation & GMP

Overview

Industrial pharmacy covers the manufacturing, formulation, quality-control, and regulatory aspects of producing medicines at scale. It includes formulation design, stability testing, scale-up, and quality assurance to ensure products meet safety and efficacy standards.

Key concepts

  • Good Manufacturing Practices (GMP) and regulatory compliance
  • Formulation and excipient selection
  • Process validation and quality control
  • Packaging, labelling and cold-chain logistics for biologics

Further reading: FDA on Current Good Manufacturing Practice (CGMP) regulations — https://www.fda.gov/drugs/pharmaceutical-quality-resources/current-good-manufacturing-practice-cgmp-regulations
