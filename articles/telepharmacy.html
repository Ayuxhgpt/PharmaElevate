Telepharmacy — Reaching Remote Patients

Overview

Telepharmacy delivers pharmaceutical care remotely using telecommunications — enabling medication review, counselling, and dispensing in underserved areas. Evidence shows telepharmacy improves access and supports medication safety when implemented with pharmacist oversight.

Guidance

ASHP and peer-reviewed reviews outline operational models and considerations for telepharmacy services.

Further reading: ASHP telepharmacy statement (PDF) and review articles on telepharmacy implementation.
