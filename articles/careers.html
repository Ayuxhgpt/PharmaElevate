Career Options After B.Pharmacy

Paths

  • Clinical pharmacist roles in hospitals and community settings
  • Industrial roles: formulation, QA/QC, regulatory affairs
  • Research & higher studies (M.Pharm, PhD) and GPAT preparation
  • Entrepreneurship: pharmacies, startups in digital health

Advice

Gain internships, build practical lab skills, and consider certifications in clinical pharmacy or regulatory affairs to improve employability.
