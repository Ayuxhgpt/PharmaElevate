How to Prepare for GPAT — Strategy

Overview

GPAT (Graduate Pharmacy Aptitude Test) tests core knowledge across pharmaceutics, pharmacology, medicinal chemistry, and related subjects. Key strategies include a study schedule, practicing previous year papers, and focusing on high-yield topics.

Study plan

  • Daily topic-wise revision + weekly mock tests
  • Analyse mistakes and maintain short revision notes
  • Practice numeric and reasoning questions under timed conditions
