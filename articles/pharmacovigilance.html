What is Pharmacovigilance? — In Depth

Definition

Pharmacovigilance is the science related to the detection, assessment, understanding and prevention of adverse effects or any other drug-related problems. It underpins drug safety monitoring and regulatory action.

Processes

  • Adverse event (AE) reporting systems and signal detection
  • Causality assessment and risk minimisation strategies
  • Regulatory reporting and communication to healthcare professionals
